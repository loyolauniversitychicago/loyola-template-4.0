Three words

LEADERSHIP. SCHOLARSHIP. SERVICE.

They are the words etched on to the award that Loyola University Chicago presents every year to its most outstanding students. Those words were chosen for the President’s Medallion because they summarize all that the University represents. They speak for the success that these students have worked so hard toward at Loyola—and the promise they carry with them after.
