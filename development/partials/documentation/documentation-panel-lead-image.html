Panels - Image Combinations

Panel - Lead Image

Direction and Guidance

Usage - Link to a timely story or content.

1Images - Use simple images with centered focal point for mobile. Formats: 1000px x 800px - 16:7 or 1800px x 1000px - 16:9.

2Label - Use a short label to add context.

3Headline - Use a short 5-10 word headline.

4Text and CTA - Use a short 10-15 word line of text with a CTA. The CTA is not required. Image Lead can be a standalone.
